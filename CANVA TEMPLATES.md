## Event Announcement — fill recipe (from Skoll / Geneva / Impact Days set)
A **3-post set per conference**, brand-color-tinted (sky `#20bcff` or orange `#f16322`):
1. **Cover ("Splash at …")** — host-city skyline photo + brand tint · Splash drop
   logo top · "Splash at [Event]" (event name/logo is an IMAGE — swap it) ·
   `[MONTH YEAR] · [CITY]` bar (TEXT) · tagline (TEXT).
2. **Speaker / Connect card** — solid brand-color bg · event logo · headshot ·
   name + title + org · pull-quote (TEXT) · `LET'S CONNECT IN [CITY]` (TEXT).
3. **Recap** — reuse In Partnership → Event Recap.

**API note:** in these templates the **event name + skyline are IMAGES (swap)**;
date/city/tagline/connect-line are **editable TEXT**. Watch text length on fixed
date bars — long strings wrap and can strand a fixed bullet separator.
